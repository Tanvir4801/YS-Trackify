CRITICAL DIFFERENCE — Advance is NOT an allowance:
- Petrol/Lunch/Breakfast/Tea ADD to payroll total
- Advance DEDUCTS from payroll total (it's a loan)
- Advance was already paid in cash → deduct at payroll time

attendance/{date}/records/{labourId}
  ADD:
  advance: number (default 0)

  grandTotal calculation:
  grandTotal = wageAtTime + totalAllowance - advance

  Show clearly in reports:
  ₹650 wage + ₹280 allowances - ₹500 advance = ₹430 net payable

SEPARATE advance ledger (important for tracking):
advances/{labourId}/entries/{entryId}
  - date: string
  - amount: number
  - siteId: string
  - givenBy: uid (supervisor)

  - linkedAttendanceDate: string
  - type: "daily" (from attendance) | "extra" (admin gives
